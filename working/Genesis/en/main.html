TWO LINES NFT

video

The TWO LINES design, which embodies the wing of the Genesis emblem, was first applied to the GV80 concept car in 2017 and has established itself as a powerful design icon of Genesis.

TWO LINES NFT contains the Genesis vehicle artwork along with the TWO LINES design that symbolizes Genesis.

Experience various missions and benefits exclusively for the TWO LINES community.

TWO LINES NFT BENEFIT

  • GEN TOKEN
    GEN TOKEN can be obtained by possessing NFT and participating in various missions. GEN TOKEN can be used to get rewards or purchase limited edition NFTs.
  • limited edition NFT
    TWO LINES NFT holders can participate in the limited edition NFT auction.
  • Reward
    Priority invitations and special programs will be provided to NFT holders to participate in various sports, motor shows, and cultural events where they can experience the uniqueness of Genesis.
  • GOVERNANCE
    TWO LINES NFT contains voting rights. TWO LINES holders can participate in key decision-making of the project.

FAQ

  • Q. How to use KLIP wallet?

    1) Click [···] at the bottom right of KakaoTalk, then click [All Services]

    2) Click [KLIP] in the lower right corner

    3) Click [Sign up with Kakao Account]

    4) Set a password for using KLIP wallet

    5) Click the [OK] button to complete KLIP registration

    KLIP registration guide download
  • Q. How to buy KLAY?

    1) Visit the Central Exchange Korbit. (Withdrawal is not possible when using other exchanges.)

    2) Search for KLAY.

    3) Enter the amount to purchase.

    4) Click the menu.

    5) Enter your KLIP wallet address and amount to receive KLAY and click the [Request Withdrawal] button.

    6) Withdrawal request will be implemented after completing Google OTP or phone verification.

    7) When withdrawing to a new wallet address, mail verification is required with the email address registered in Korbit.

    8) Withdrawal will be completed after all verification process.

    ※ It may take up to 48 hours after the withdrawal request is completed.

  • Q. How can I get GEN TOKEN?

    You can obtain GEN TOKEN by staking NFTs and participating in missions. GEN TOKEN can be used to purchase rewards and limited edition NFTs.

  • Q. How can I purchase LIMITED EDITION NFT?

    Only TWO LINES holders can participate in LIMITED EDITION NFT auction event, and you can offer an auction price with GEN TOKEN. At the end of the auction period, the participant who offered the highest price will receive the LIMITED EDITION NFT.

  • Q. How can I participate in GOVERNANCE?

    You can obtain voting rights by staking TWO LINES NFTs in MY NFT menu. In the GOVERNANCE menu, you can participate in key decision-making by voting.
